Bubble Grader

To learn more about how the grader works, check out my blog post.

Instructions:
1. Print the NCAA_quiz.pdf and 10_question_bubble_sheet.pdf found here.
2. Bubble in the answers.
3. Snap a photo of the quiz with your camera, and we'll use computer vision to grade it!

Here are a few tips for getting the best results with the photo:

  • Use a pen to fill in the bubbles (sharper contrast)
  • The bubble sheet should take up the majority of the image, while still capturing the edges completely
  • Use a plain background behind the piece of paper
  • Turning on the flash can help reduce shadows and lighting variation
